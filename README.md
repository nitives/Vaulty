## Features

- **Local-first**: All your data is stored locally on your device.
- **Easy to use**: Simply paste or drag and drop content to create a new item.
- **Tag-based organization**: Keep your items organized with tags.
- **Search**: Find your items quickly with powerful search `Ctrl+F`.
- **Customizable**: Customize the look and feel of the app.
